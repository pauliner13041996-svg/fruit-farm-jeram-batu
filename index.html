Dine, Cook & Celebrate on the Farm

More than a farm — join us for an unforgettable outdoor dining experience powered by our authentic wood-fired clay oven.

Our signature mosaic clay oven lets you grill chicken, roast vegetables, and cook authentic BBQ meals right in the heart of the farm. Pure wood fire flavour.

Artisan Bread Baking

Bake your own rustic sourdough bread in the wood-fired oven — fresh, warm, and crispy. A truly hands-on farm activity for all ages.

Dine under string lights surrounded by coconut palms. Our outdoor area creates a magical atmosphere for groups, families, and events.
