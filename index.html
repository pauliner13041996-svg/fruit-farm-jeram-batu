Farm Experience & Dining

More than a farm — come for an unforgettable outdoor dining experience powered by our authentic wood-fired oven.

Wood-Fired Oven BBQ

Our signature mosaic clay oven lets you grill chicken, roast vegetables, and cook authentic BBQ meals right in the heart of the farm. No gas — pure wood fire flavour.

Artisan Bread Baking

Experience baking your own rustic sourdough bread in the wood-fired oven. Fresh out of the oven, warm and crispy — a truly hands-on farm activity.
