About Us

A family legacy rooted in the fertile land of Jeram Batu.

Our Story

Fruit Farm Jeram Batu was founded over two decades ago by a family deeply passionate about the land. What started as a small plot of guava and banana trees has grown into one of the region's most beloved tropical fruit farms.

Located in the lush lowlands of Jeram Batu, Selangor, our farm benefits from rich alluvial soil and a warm, humid climate — perfect conditions for growing an abundance of sweet, juicy tropical fruits.

We believe that the best fruits come from healthy soil, clean water, and the kind of patient care that only a family business can provide. Three generations later, that belief drives everything we do.
