Our Story

Who We Are

Two Decades of Growing the Finest Tropical Fruits

Fruit Farm Jeram Batu was founded over two decades ago by a family deeply passionate about the land. What started as a small coconut plantation has grown into one of Johor's most beloved tropical fruit farms, with coconut remaining our pride and main crop.

Located in the fertile lowlands of Jeram Batu, Pekan Nenas, Johor, our farm benefits from rich alluvial soil, abundant rainfall, and a warm tropical climate — perfect conditions for our thriving coconut palms and a wide variety of other tropical fruits.

We believe that the best produce comes from healthy soil, clean water, and patient care that only a family business can provide. Every coconut and every fruit that leaves our farm carries that commitment.
